What Cooling System Repair Involves

Diagnosis: We begin with a visual inspection, checking coolant level, condition, and concentration. We perform a cooling system pressure test to identify external leaks, check thermostat operation, test the cooling fans, and if needed, perform a combustion leak test to check for head gasket issues.

Repair: Based on diagnosis, we replace the failed component using OEM-spec parts. For coolant service, we properly drain the old coolant, flush any contamination, and refill with the exact type and concentration specified for your vehicle. We always bleed the system to remove air pockets that could cause hot spots.

Verification: We run the engine to operating temperature, verify the thermostat opens, check for leaks under pressure, and confirm the cooling fans activate at the correct temperature.
