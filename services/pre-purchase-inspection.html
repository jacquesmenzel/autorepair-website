Why Florida Cars Need Extra Scrutiny

South Florida's environment creates unique risks for used car buyers:

  • Flood damage: Florida is the #1 state for hurricane-damaged vehicles being resold. We check for hidden water damage that a title wash may have concealed.
  • Salt air corrosion: Coastal vehicles in Fort Lauderdale and Tampa Bay develop undercarriage corrosion that's invisible from the surface.
  • Heat damage: Extreme heat degrades rubber components, batteries, and interior materials faster than cooler climates.
  • UV damage: Florida's intense sun fades paint, cracks dashboards, and deteriorates weather stripping.
