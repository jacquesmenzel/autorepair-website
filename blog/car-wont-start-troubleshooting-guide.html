What to Do When Your Car Won't Start

You turn the key (or push the button) and... nothing. Before you panic, here's a quick troubleshooting guide.

Scenario 1: Nothing Happens â€” No Sound

Likely cause: Dead battery, loose connection, or faulty ignition switch. Try turning on headlights â€” if they don't work, it's a battery issue.

Scenario 2: Clicking Sound

Likely cause: A single loud click usually points to the starter. Rapid clicking suggests a weak battery. Try a jump start.

Scenario 3: Engine Cranks but Won't Fire

Likely cause: Fuel system, ignition, or sensor issue. Check fuel level and listen for the fuel pump whir when turning to "ON".

Scenario 4: Starts Then Immediately Dies

Likely cause: Idle air control, throttle body, or anti-theft system. Try your spare key if the security light is flashing.

Scenario 5: Grinding Noise

Likely cause: Starter gear not engaging with flywheel. Don't keep trying â€” you can damage the flywheel. This needs professional repair.
