Quality of Work

Dealership techs are trained on your specific brand but are often incentivized to upsell. A good mobile mechanic uses OEM or quality aftermarket parts with a warranty. Our team has 20+ years of experience across all makes and models.

Convenience

This is where mobile mechanics win decisively. Zero travel time, zero waiting room time. Your car gets fixed while you work or relax.

When to Choose the Dealership

  • Warranty work
  • Manufacturer recalls
  • Highly specialized brand-specific issues

When to Choose a Mobile Mechanic

  • Routine maintenance
  • Your car won't start
  • You value your time
  • Most common repairs

The Verdict: For 80-90% of auto repairs, a qualified mobile mechanic delivers the same quality at a better price with dramatically better convenience.
